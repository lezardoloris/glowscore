Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

GlowUp AI ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

Photos: When you use GlowUp AI, you provide photos (selfies) for AI transformation.

  • Free tier: Photos are processed entirely on your device using on-device machine learning. They are never uploaded to any server.
  • Premium tier (HD): Photos are encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3), sent to our secure edge servers (Cloudflare Workers) for AI processing via fal.ai, and permanently deleted within 24 hours. We do not store, sell, or use your photos for any purpose other than generating your transformation.

Subscription Data: Managed by RevenueCat and Apple. We receive anonymized subscription status (active/inactive) but never see your payment details.

Analytics: We collect anonymized usage data (screens viewed, features used, crash reports) via Firebase Analytics to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Device Identifiers: We may collect anonymized device identifiers for analytics purposes only. We do not use these for advertising or tracking across apps.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-powered photo transformations
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To send you weekly new style notifications (only if you opt in)

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • fal.ai: Processes your photos for HD transformations (premium only). Subject to their privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare: Hosts our edge API. Temporary image storage auto-deletes after 24 hours.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription state. No photo data shared.
  • Firebase (Google): Anonymized analytics and crash reporting only.

4. Data Retention

  • Photos (server): Deleted within 24 hours of processing
  • Photos (device): Stored locally in your transformation history until you delete them
  • Analytics: Retained for 14 months (Firebase default)
  • Subscription data: Retained by RevenueCat per their retention policy

5. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all data: Use Settings → Delete All Data to permanently remove all locally stored transformations and usage data
  • Opt out of notifications: Disable in iOS Settings → GlowUp AI → Notifications
  • Request data deletion: Contact us at support@glowupai.app

6. Children's Privacy

GlowUp AI is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, contact us immediately.

7. Biometric Data

GlowUp AI uses Apple's Vision framework for face detection (on-device only). Face detection data is processed locally and never transmitted. We do not create or store biometric templates, facial recognition profiles, or faceprints.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via notification.
