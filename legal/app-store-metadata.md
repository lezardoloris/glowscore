## Review Notes for Apple

GlowUp AI is an entertainment app that generates AI artistic visualizations from user selfies.

Key points for review:
1. All AI-generated results include an "artistic visualization" disclaimer on every result screen and in Settings
2. Free tier processes photos entirely on-device (no server upload). Premium tier uploads securely to Cloudflare Workers for HD processing; images are deleted within 24 hours
3. The paywall includes full subscription terms, auto-renewal disclosure, Terms of Use, Privacy Policy, and Restore Purchases as required by guideline 3.1.2
4. No user accounts or sign-up required — the app uses anonymous RevenueCat identifiers
5. Face detection uses Apple's Vision framework (on-device only)
6. The app does NOT claim to improve appearance — all language uses "artistic visualization" and "creative exploration"

To test the app:
- Free tier: Open app → Take/select photo → Choose "Clear Skin" (free) → View result with watermark
- Premium features are gated behind the paywall (no demo account needed — subscription is the only gated content)
