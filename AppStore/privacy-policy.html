Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

GlowUp AI ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

What We Collect

  • Photos: When you use GlowUp AI, your photo is sent to our AI processing service (fal.ai) to generate your transformation. Photos are processed and deleted within 24 hours. We do not permanently store your original or transformed photos on our servers.
  • Subscription Data: Subscription purchases are managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We receive an anonymous subscriber ID to validate your subscription status. We do not see your payment details.
  • Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data (screens viewed, features used, error rates) to improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, or personal information
  • We do not use facial recognition or store biometric data
  • We do not sell or share your photos with third parties
  • We do not use your photos for AI training

How We Process Photos

  1. Your photo is sent encrypted (HTTPS) to our edge server (Cloudflare Workers)
  2. The edge server forwards it to fal.ai for AI transformation
  3. The generated result is returned to your device
  4. Both original and generated images are deleted from servers within 24 hours
  5. All generated images are stored locally on your device only

Third-Party Services

  • fal.ai: AI image processing. fal.ai Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Firebase: Anonymous analytics and crash reporting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare: Edge computing and CDN. Cloudflare Privacy Policy

Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to:

  • Request information about what data we hold
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics (contact us)

Since we don't collect personal data or store photos, there is typically no data to delete. If you have concerns, contact us.

Children's Privacy

GlowUp AI is rated 12+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Disclaimer

GlowUp AI creates artistic visualizations using AI. Results are creative interpretations for entertainment and creative exploration purposes only. They should not be taken as realistic predictions of appearance.
