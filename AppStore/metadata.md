Discover your Facial Harmony in seconds.

GlowScore is your personal AI beauty analyst. Scan your face and get a clear, clinical-style read on your facial harmony, then unlock a personalized glow-up plan to bring out your best self.

WHAT YOU GET:
- Facial Harmony score with a detailed breakdown across 6 areas: Skin Clarity, Facial Symmetry, Nose & Profile, Eye Area, Jawline Definition, and Lips & Smile
- Your personalized glow-up plan: simple, non-invasive skincare, makeup, grooming and lighting tips tailored to your scan
- See Your Maxed-Out Self: a photorealistic preview of your glow-up potential
- Weekly progress re-scans so you can track your glow over time and build a streak
- Beautiful before/after cards to share with friends

WHY GLOWSCORE:
GlowScore is built to be encouraging, never harsh. Every score is framed as an opportunity, with constructive, positive guidance. It is designed for entertainment and self-care, not as a medical or factual judgment of worth.
